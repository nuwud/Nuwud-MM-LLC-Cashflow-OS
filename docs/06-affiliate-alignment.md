# Affiliate Alignment Strategy

Affiliate marketing is a secondary revenue layer.

It should never make Nuwud look like a coupon farm, tool shill, or low-trust internet marketer.

## Strategic role

Affiliate recommendations should appear as part of:

- audit findings
- tool-stack recommendations
- founder systems education
- resource pages
- comparison posts
- implementation checklists
- automation tutorials
- Shopify / WordPress / AI workflow content

## Best-fit affiliate categories

### 1. Ecommerce platform / ecosystem

Best for Shopify strategy, migrations, audits, and app ecosystem content.

### 2. Hosting / infrastructure

Best for WordPress clients, speed audits, security, reliability, and migration conversations.

### 3. CRM / marketing automation

Best for lead follow-up, email flows, founder pipelines, sales automation, and lifecycle systems.

### 4. SEO / AI visibility tools

Best for authority content, audits, content systems, and visibility strategy.

### 5. Automation tools

Best for n8n workflows, AI operations, internal systems, and workflow education.

### 6. Analytics / CRO / ecommerce apps

Best for Shopify audits and revenue leak maps.

## Content rules

Every affiliate post should satisfy at least one of these:

- explains a real business problem
- teaches a repeatable framework
- shows how to choose a tool
- compares options honestly
- connects the tool to a larger Nuwud system

## Disclosure language

Use simple, direct disclosure.

Examples:

> Disclosure: I may earn a commission if you buy through my link. I only recommend tools that fit the system I’m describing.

> Affiliate note: Some links may compensate Nuwud Multimedia LLC. The recommendation is based on fit, not the commission.

## Approved content angles

- "The tool is not the system. Here is where it fits."
- "Before you rebuild your website, check these revenue leaks."
- "A founder’s stack should reduce decisions, not add noise."
- "Why your follow-up system matters more than your homepage."
- "How I would structure the first automation layer for a founder-led business."
- "The difference between buying software and installing a revenue system."

## Avoid

- fake urgency
- income claims
- unverified results
- hidden affiliate links
- affiliate links in every post
- recommending tools before diagnosing the business
- comparing tools you have not fairly evaluated

## Important distinction

For client implementation work, affiliate revenue is gravy.

The main money remains:

```text
Audit -> implementation -> retainer -> referral
```

Affiliate links should increase trust and optionality, not replace premium services.
