# The Nuwud Money Loop

This is the core operating loop.

Run it daily until cashflow stabilizes.

## Loop overview

```text
1. Capture
2. Qualify
3. Open conversation
4. Diagnose lightly
5. Offer paid audit
6. Invoice / collect
7. Deliver audit
8. Propose implementation
9. Collect deposit
10. Document proof
11. Follow-up / retainer / referral
```

## 1. Capture

Every possible buyer goes into the tracker.

Sources:

- past clients
- warm friends / legacy clients
- Shopify brands
- WordPress businesses with visible issues
- local premium service businesses
- subscription brands
- businesses with outdated sites but real operations
- founder-led companies posting pain on LinkedIn or X
- referrals from existing relationships

Minimum fields:

- Business / person
- Website
- Contact method
- Pain observed
- Offer angle
- Status
- Next action
- Next action date
- Potential value

## 2. Qualify

Use quick scoring.

| Signal | Question |
| --- | --- |
| Business reality | Do they already make money or have real operations? |
| Pain | Is there a visible leak in leads, conversion, ops, ecommerce, follow-up, or AI adoption? |
| Authority | Can Patrick reach the decision maker? |
| Urgency | Is there a reason to act soon? |
| Fit | Does the work fit Nuwud systems architecture? |
| Ability to pay | Can they reasonably pay at least $1,500 for diagnosis? |

Avoid low-trust, low-budget, vague prospects unless they are warm and strategic.

## 3. Open conversation

The goal is not to pitch immediately.

The goal is to create a relevant business conversation.

Good openers:

- "I noticed something on your site that may be costing leads."
- "This looks like a revenue leak, not just a website issue."
- "You may not need a rebuild first. You may need the system mapped."
- "I can take a paid diagnostic pass and show you exactly what I would fix first."

## 4. Diagnose lightly

Free diagnosis should be shallow enough to create urgency, not deep enough to replace the audit.

Free:

- identify likely problem
- explain possible impact
- name the category of fix
- invite paid audit

Paid:

- full teardown
- prioritization
- screenshots
- tool recommendations
- implementation roadmap
- cost estimates
- automation plan
- affiliate/tool stack where appropriate

## 5. Offer paid audit

Default line:

> I can do a paid Revenue Systems Audit where I map the leaks, rank the fixes, and give you a clear action plan before you spend on a rebuild or automation stack.

Price anchors:

- Friendly / legacy investigation: $1,500
- Standard audit: $2,500
- Premium public-facing audit: $2,997
- Urgent / complex diagnostic: $3,500+

## 6. Invoice / collect

No audit starts without payment or a confirmed deposit.

Payment language:

> Once the invoice is handled, I can reserve the time and give this the proper diagnostic pass instead of trying to piece it together between other obligations.

## 7. Deliver audit

Standard deliverable:

1. Executive summary
2. Current-state assessment
3. Revenue leaks
4. Conversion / UX issues
5. Technical risks
6. Automation / AI opportunities
7. Tool-stack recommendations
8. Priority roadmap
9. Implementation options

## 8. Propose implementation

Every audit should naturally lead to one clear implementation recommendation.

Implementation options:

| Tier | Use when | Price |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Revenue Repair Sprint | focused fixes, quick ROI | $5,000–$7,500 |
| Founder Systems Buildout | full funnel/site/automation system | $10,000–$20,000 |
| AI-Native Growth Infrastructure | complex Shopify/AI/ops architecture | $25,000–$60,000+ |
| Retainer | ongoing optimization and support | $1,000+/month |

## 9. Document proof

After every win, capture:

- before state
- what was changed
- result or expected business impact
- client quote if possible
- screenshot / artifact
- anonymized lesson for content

## 10. Repeat

The loop only works when repeated.

Daily target:

- 3 to 5 sales actions before builder work

Weekly target:

- 20 to 50 new/warm prospects touched
- 3 to 5 audit offers made
- 1 to 3 invoices sent
