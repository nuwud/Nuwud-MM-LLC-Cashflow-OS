# Nuwud Offer Ladder

The offer ladder keeps Nuwud from jumping straight into huge custom builds without payment, scope clarity, or momentum.

## Front-door offer

# Nuwud Revenue Systems Audit

A paid diagnostic that finds where a founder-led business is leaking leads, sales, conversion, follow-up, automation leverage, and operational clarity.

## Promise

Find the hidden leaks in the website, offer, automation, ecommerce flow, and follow-up system, then deliver a prioritized plan for turning the digital presence into revenue infrastructure.

## Who it is for

- Shopify brands with traffic or sales but weak conversion
- WordPress businesses with outdated sites and weak lead capture
- local premium service businesses that need better qualified leads
- subscription businesses with retention or conversion issues
- founder-led companies overwhelmed by tools and AI noise
- businesses that need systems before another redesign

## Who it is not for

- people shopping for the cheapest website
- businesses with no ability to pay for implementation
- vague idea-stage projects without urgency
- clients who expect deep strategy for free

## Audit deliverables

- Executive summary
- Revenue leak map
- Website / UX / conversion review
- Offer clarity review
- Technical / platform risk notes
- Automation and AI leverage map
- Recommended tool stack
- Affiliate/resource recommendations where appropriate
- Priority roadmap
- Implementation options

## Implementation ladder

## Revenue Repair Sprint

**Price:** $5,000–$7,500

**Use when:** client needs focused fixes that can be shipped quickly.

Examples:

- landing page repair
- checkout / product page improvement
- lead capture and follow-up fix
- analytics and tracking repair
- Shopify section/system cleanup
- WordPress performance / conversion improvements

## Founder Systems Buildout

**Price:** $10,000–$20,000

**Use when:** client needs a fuller business system.

Examples:

- Shopify or WordPress rebuild with conversion architecture
- sales funnel + lead intake + CRM automation
- service offer architecture
- onboarding flow
- content-to-lead system
- founder dashboard

## AI-Native Growth Infrastructure

**Price:** $25,000–$60,000+

**Use when:** client has serious scale, complexity, or opportunity.

Examples:

- AI-assisted operations stack
- automated lead qualification and routing
- ecommerce personalization / data workflows
- internal knowledge systems
- 3D/immersive ecommerce experience
- advanced Shopify/Hydrogen architecture

## Systems Optimization Retainer

**Price:** $1,000+/month

**Use when:** client needs ongoing support, optimization, and systems maintenance.

Possible retainers:

- $1,000/mo light optimization and priority support
- $2,500/mo growth systems support
- $5,000+/mo strategic systems partner

## Offer discipline

Never let the implementation conversation replace the paid audit.

Correct path:

```text
Problem noticed -> paid audit -> implementation recommendation -> deposit -> delivery
```

Wrong path:

```text
Problem noticed -> unpaid deep analysis -> custom plan -> client delays -> no cash
```
