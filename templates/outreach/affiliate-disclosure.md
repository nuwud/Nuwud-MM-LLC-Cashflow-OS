# Affiliate Disclosure Templates

Use clear disclosure any time affiliate links may compensate Nuwud Multimedia LLC.

## Short disclosure

Affiliate note: Nuwud Multimedia LLC may earn a commission if you buy through this link. I only recommend tools that fit the system I’m describing.

## More formal disclosure

Disclosure: Some links may be affiliate links, which means Nuwud Multimedia LLC may earn a commission if you purchase through them. Recommendations are based on strategic fit, client usefulness, and system alignment — not just commission potential.

## Social post disclosure

Affiliate note: I may earn a commission from this recommendation. The tool still has to earn its place in the system.

## Resource page disclosure

Some resources listed on this page may include affiliate links. That means Nuwud Multimedia LLC may receive compensation if you sign up through those links. I recommend tools based on whether they help founder-led businesses build clearer, stronger revenue infrastructure.

## Audit/report disclosure

Some optional tool recommendations may include affiliate relationships. These recommendations are included because they appear relevant to the business problem identified in this audit. You are not required to use any affiliate link, and tool selection should always follow the actual implementation plan.
