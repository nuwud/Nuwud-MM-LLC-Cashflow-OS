# Warm Follow-Up Templates

## Friendly materials follow-up

Hey [Name], just checking in on the materials/info I need from you. Once I have that, I can put together the complete offer, pricing, and next steps properly instead of guessing.

## Payment gate follow-up

Hey [Name], I’m ready to move the next part forward, but I need to get the billing cleaned up first so I can allocate the proper time and tools to it. I’ll send over the invoice for the recent work / next diagnostic step. Once that’s handled, I can get this organized and moving without piecing it together.

## Paid audit offer

I can take a paid diagnostic pass on this and map out what’s actually leaking money, what needs fixing first, and what I’d recommend before you spend on a rebuild or a bigger system. That keeps it structured instead of us guessing our way through it.

## Legacy client boundary

Because we’ve worked together a long time, I want to keep this clean and fair. I can help, but I need to separate quick conversation from actual diagnostic/implementation work so neither of us gets stuck in vague scope.

## Soft reactivation

Hey [Name], I was looking back at the work we did and had a thought: there may be a bigger systems opportunity now than just site updates. A lot of businesses are leaking money through weak follow-up, unclear offers, and disconnected tools. I’m doing paid Revenue Systems Audits now if you want me to take a structured look.
